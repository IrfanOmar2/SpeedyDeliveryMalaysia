Job Description

POSITION SUMMARY
Speedy Delivery is seeking for Commercial (Telesales) Executives that are convincing and can show potential and existing partners that they have great selling, networking, and closing skills. The Telesales Executive is in charge of a top strategy of phoning, pitching, following up, and servicing partners.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
While leads will be provided, you will be required to:

  • Have a natural aptitude to sell over the phone.
  • Be relentless in your efforts to find, contact, and speak with the right decision makers in a company.
  • To meet KPIs, be sales-minded and resourceful.
  • Comply with paperwork accuracy, partner approval, and system processing criteria.
  • Keep track of calls and sales, and jot down useful information in a neat and orderly manner.
  • A aptitude for managing objections, being persuasive, establishing urgency, and moving quickly.
  • Problem solving is second nature to us, and we're always aiming to give solutions that are in line with our company's offerings.
  • Consistently meeting and exceeding the company's key performance indicators (KPIs).

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Because this is a 100% telesales position, the candidate must be capable of making outbound calls using digital communication tools and being on the phone for long periods of time.
  • Able to converse effectively in Malay and English.
  • Has prior sales/telesales/telemarketing/outbound call experience.
  • Experienced with internet business tools, such as Google apps. Salesforce experience is a plus, but it is not required.
  • An excellent communicator who takes a consultative sales approach.
  • An excellent relationship builder who is goal-oriented, resilient, and a team player who is eager to grow.
  • Quick learner who can swiftly adjust to changes in operational processes.
