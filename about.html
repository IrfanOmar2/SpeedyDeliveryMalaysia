About Us

With one objective in mind from the beginning, Speedy Delivery set out to become the most convenient meal delivery business in the country. Years later, it is still doing strong in Malaysia, with operations in more than 12 states and a large number of satisfied consumers. Anyone can select their favourite restaurants near them and place orders for their favourite dishes, as well as snacks, beverages, and other items, using the simple smartphone app or the website.

Whether you're looking for breakfast delivery, lunch delivery, or supper delivery, Speedy Delivery is your one-stop shop for ordering food from any type of restaurant or shop in Malaysia. We believe that meals should be a pleasure, and that ordering food should be a quick and enjoyable experience.
